# IIVCS Feedback System

**Operational Feedback | Organizational Feedback | Strategic Reassessment**

The IIVCS feedback system is how the organization continuously refines where and how it creates value — surfacing signals from the customer journey, translating them into organizational outcomes, and returning them upstream to drive strategic reassessment. Feedback operates at two levels: operational, through CVDM's five feedback loops; and organizational, through the 8 Values metrics that surface outcomes back to OSM.

---

## The Two Levels

**Operational feedback** surfaces at the journey and value stream level — CVDM's five continuous feedback loops capture signals from customer experience, journey insights, delivery learning, strategic evolution, and performance optimization. These loops feed PTS and OSM with signals about how value is being delivered and received.

**Organizational feedback** surfaces at the organizational level — the 8 Values metrics aggregate the outcomes of strategy, transformation, and delivery into organizational dimensions that feed back into OSM's Governance & Measurement and strategic reassessment.

Together, the two levels form the complete upstream feedback path: CVDM → PTS → OSM.

---

## Operational Feedback — CVDM

CVDM's five continuous feedback loops are the operational feedback mechanism of the IIVCS — surfacing signals across Efficiency, Experience, and Effectiveness at the journey and value stage level.

| Feedback Loop | What It Surfaces |
|---|---|
| Experience feedback | How the customer felt at each journey step — the first and most immediate signal |
| Journey insights | Where friction, gaps, and misalignments exist across the journey |
| Delivery learning | Whether value stages are delivering intended customer outcomes |
| Strategic evolution | Whether strategic priorities remain valid given delivery outcomes |
| Performance optimization | Whether capabilities and processes are performing at the required maturity |

Experience feedback surfaces first — the most immediate signal from the customer journey. Delivery learning surfaces last — the outcome validation that feeds strategic reassessment.

---

## Organizational Feedback — 8 Values

The 8 Values metrics aggregate organizational outcomes and return them upstream to OSM's Governance & Measurement — validating whether strategic priorities are generating the right organizational value across all eight dimensions.

| 8 Value | What It Signals |
|---|---|
| **Customer Value** | Whether strategic customer priorities are generating adoption, engagement, and retention |
| **Financial Value** | Whether strategic investment is generating current and forward-looking financial returns |
| **People Value** | Whether the operating model is building the human capability and engagement the system needs |
| **Operational Value** | Whether the product operating model is performing at the required capability and execution quality |
| **Market Value** | Whether strategic direction is building the brand, reputation, and market position intended |
| **Strategic Value** | Whether strategy formulation is clear, executable, and generating the outcomes hypothesized |
| **Innovation Value** | Whether investment in future value streams is adequate and generating the pipeline needed |
| **Impact Value** | Whether the organization is advancing its mission and living its values through delivery |

People Value and Operational Value feed PTS — signaling whether the operating model needs adjustment. All other Values feed OSM — directly informing Governance & Measurement and strategic reassessment.

---

## The Complete Feedback Path

The IIVCS feedback system runs upstream in two layers — operational signals rising first, organizational signals confirming last.

```
CVDM      Experience → Journey insights → Delivery learning → Strategic evolution
                ↓
AER       Adoption → Engagement → Retention
                ↓
8 Values  Customer → Financial → Market → Strategic → Innovation → Impact
                ↓
OSM       Governance & Measurement → Strategic Reassessment
```

3E provides the measurement language at every point in the return journey — grounding both operational and organizational feedback in Efficiency, Experience, and Effectiveness.

The feedback runs upstream — CVDM to PTS to OSM — customer signals becoming delivery learning, delivery learning becoming strategic evolution, continuously refining where the organization creates value.

---

## Summary

The IIVCS feedback system ensures the organization never loses contact with reality — at the journey level or the organizational level.

Operational feedback keeps PTS connected to customer experience and delivery quality. Organizational feedback keeps OSM connected to the outcomes its strategic priorities are — or are not — generating. Together they form the continuous learning loop that makes the IIVCS a living system, not a static framework.
