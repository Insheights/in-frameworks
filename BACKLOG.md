# Insheights Value Creation Suite — Backlog

---

## High Priority

### 1. Practitioners Guide — OSP
A step-by-step guide for practitioners applying the Organization Strategy Planning framework. Covers how to run the strategy development process, align stakeholders, translate Vision into Strategic Objectives, and set up Governance & Measurement cadences.

### 2. Practitioners Guide — PTS
A step-by-step guide for practitioners applying the Product Transformation System. Covers how to stand up a Product Operating Model, structure cross-functional teams around value streams, activate each POM Enabler, and run the Discover–Support phases continuously.

### 3. Practitioners Guide — CVDM
A step-by-step guide for practitioners applying the Customer Value Delivery Model. Covers how to map customer journeys to value streams, define value stages, operationalize capabilities and processes, and activate the five feedback loops.

### 4. Value Stream Design Guide
Guidance on how to identify, define, and structure value streams for a given organization. Value streams are the central organizing concept across all three frameworks — OSP aligns priorities to them, PTS organizes teams around them, CVDM delivers through them.

### 5. Adoption Sequencing & Maturity Model
A maturity model that helps organizations self-assess their current state and determine where to start — OSP, PTS, or CVDM — and in what sequence. Defines what early, developing, and mature adoption looks like across the suite.

---

## Medium Priority

### 6. Measurement Framework
A shared measurement model spanning all three frameworks. Defines what good looks like at each layer — strategic, product, and customer — and identifies the signals and metrics that indicate the suite is working, not just being followed.

### 7. Role and Ownership Model
Defines who drives OSP, who operationalizes PTS, and who governs CVDM. Covers decision rights, responsibilities, and how leadership and teams interact across the three frameworks.

---

## Lower Priority

### 8. Integration with Existing Frameworks
Guidance on how the Value Creation Suite sits alongside or replaces existing methods organizations already use — including SAFe, Scrum, OKRs, and ITIL. Reduces adoption friction for organizations that are not starting from scratch.

### 9. AI Augmentation Specifics
Detailed guidance on how AI fits into each framework — scenario modeling and risk analysis in OSP, telemetry, prioritization, and decision support in PTS, journey personalization and signal detection in CVDM.
